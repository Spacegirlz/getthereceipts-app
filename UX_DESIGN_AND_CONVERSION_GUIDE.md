### **Detailed UX Audit Prompt**

```
You are a senior UX designer specializing in landing page optimization.

Please conduct a comprehensive UX audit of the landing page:

1. VISUAL HIERARCHY:
   - Is the most important information most prominent?
   - Are CTAs clear and well-positioned?
   - Does the eye flow naturally through the page?

2. COGNITIVE LOAD:
   - Is there too much information at once?
   - Are sections too dense?
   - Can users quickly understand the value proposition?

3. WHITESPACE & SPACING:
   - Is there enough breathing room?
   - Do sections feel cramped or spacious?
   - Are margins and padding consistent?

4. TYPOGRAPHY:
   - Is text scannable?
   - Are font sizes appropriate for hierarchy?
   - Is line-height comfortable for reading?

5. COLOR & CONTRAST:
   - Is there sufficient contrast for readability?
   - Do colors guide attention effectively?
   - Is the color palette cohesive?

6. MOBILE EXPERIENCE:
   - How does it feel on mobile?
   - Is content appropriately sized?
   - Are interactions touch-friendly?

7. COMPARISON TO BEST PRACTICES:
   - How does it compare to award-winning SaaS landing pages?
   - What patterns are missing?
   - What can be learned from top performers?

Please provide specific, actionable recommendations with examples.
```
