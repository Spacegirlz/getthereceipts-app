## **Our New Color System**

### **1. Text Hierarchy (80/20 Rule)**

**80% Neutral (White/Gray):**
- All body text: `text-white` or `text-gray-300`
- All headings: `text-white` (with weight for hierarchy)
- All descriptions: `text-gray-300` or `text-gray-400`
- All secondary text: `text-gray-400`

**20% Strategic Color:**
- **Hero headline gradient**: Keep (only place for full gradient)
- **Primary CTA buttons**: Keep gradient (cyan)
- **Icons**: Keep colored (cyan/purple/emerald for visual interest)
- **Borders/accents**: Keep colored (subtle, not overwhelming)

### **2. Color Usage Rules**

‚úÖ **DO Use Color For:**
- Hero headline (gradient text)
- Primary CTA buttons (gradient backgrounds)
- Icons (small, visual interest)
- Borders/glows (subtle accents)
- Badges (small, contained)

‚ùå **DON'T Use Color For:**
- Body text (use white/gray)
- Headings (use white with weight)
- Descriptions (use gray-300/400)
- Stats/numbers (use white)
- Feature lists (use white/gray)

### **3. Strategic Color Breaks (Gen Z Appeal)**

**Without Pink (Male-Friendly):**
- **Cyan**: Trust, tech, primary actions
- **Purple**: Premium, Sage personality
- **Emerald**: Success, free tier
- **Yellow/Amber**: Attention, urgency (sparingly)

**Color Placement:**
- Icons: Colored for quick scanning
- Borders: Colored for section breaks
- CTAs: Gradient for action
- Badges: Colored backgrounds with white text
